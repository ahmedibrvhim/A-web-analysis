Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Lower Third Calculator respects your privacy. Here's the short version of how we handle your data.

Your Photos

Your photos are processed entirely in your own browser — they're never uploaded to or stored on our servers. If you're signed in, only the numeric results (ratios, angles, scores) are saved to your account, never the photos themselves.

Account Data

If you create an account, we use Google Firebase to store your email, display name, and saved results. We never sell or share this data with third parties for marketing.

Cookies & Ads

We use local storage for basic preferences like your language choice. This Site may show ads via Google AdSense, which may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing. You can opt out anytime at Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info/choices.

Third-Party Services

We rely on Google Firebase (auth, database, hosting) and Google Fonts. Data these services process on our behalf is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You can request access to, or deletion of, your account and saved results at any time — just reach out (contact below). This Site isn't intended for anyone under 16.
